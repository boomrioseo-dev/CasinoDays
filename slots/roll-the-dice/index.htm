How to Win and Bonus Features on Roll the Dice

Adding to the fun of this slot are a few features that can trigger when spinning the reels.

2-Way Pay

The white dice acts as the Wild. If you land three or more, you will activate the 2-Way Pay feature. You will receive 10 free spins, and all wins will be doubled. If you land another three Wilds, you can retrigger the feature. 

Wild Multiplier

The red dice with x7 on it acts as the multiplier. It will award you a 7x multiplier when it joins part of a winning combination. 

Free Spins Feature

If you land three scatters, you will receive 10 free spins. You can retrigger if more scatters land during the free spins. 

Gamble Feature

On any of your winning spins in the base game, you can choose to collect your winning or gamble for double your win by selecting the correct card suit or colour. If you get it wrong, you don’t collect any winnings.
