How to Play Jhandi Munda

Paylines and Symbols

Jhandi Munda isn’t a slot game, so it has no paylines. Instead, the game’s outcome is decided on a roll of six dice with symbols. You can bet on as many symbols as you like.

Each die has six symbols: a club, flag, spade, diamond, heart, and crown. On the main board, these symbols pay at 3:2 for 1 match. The side bets differ on payouts depending on your bet.

Volatility and RTP

Jhandi Munda is a high-volatility game with an RTP of 95%, slightly below average. The high volatility is due to the nature of the game, as it is completely random, with no strategy or skill needed to play.

How to Win at Jhandi Munda and Bonus Features

Jhandi Munda is a random game based on the roll of the dice. While it is possible to bet on every single outcome, this would be a terrible strategy as it will cost you more than you could possibly win.
