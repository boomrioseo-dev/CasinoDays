How to Play Dragon Tiger Live Casino Game at Casino Days

Dragon Tiger’s gameplay is as straightforward as it gets: 

  1. The dealer draws two cards – They are placed face down with one on the Dragon side and the other on the Tiger side.
  2. Place your bet – It’s up to you to guess which card has a higher value. You can also bet on a tie to win if you think both sides have same-value cards.
  3. Choose your side bet(s) – Decide if you will take a risk for extra winnings with one of the available side bets.
  4. Wait for the results – The dealer will reveal the cards and announce the winning side. If you are right, you can win up to 1,000x your stake! If the round ends in a tie, you receive 50% of your wager amount.
