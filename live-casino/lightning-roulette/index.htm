Bonus Rounds/Games

The selection of the lightning numbers is the only bonus round in this live dealer game. During this round, up to five single numbers are selected and given a multiplier, as we explained previously. It allows you to win up to 500 times your bet. However, you have the opportunity to get that multiplier only if you bet on one of the lucky numbers and the ball lands on that number. 

For instance, you bet on 10, and it turns out to be one of the lucky selected options with a 100x multiplier. The ball then lands on 10. This means you win 100x your bet. Other wagering options like split, dozens, and high/low don’t qualify for the multiplier.
