Bonus Rounds/Games

Landing on regular numbers gets you a decent payout, but the main fun begins when the pointer hits a bonus game. Note that you must bet on a bonus game to be able to participate in it. For instance, if the puck stops on a bonus game but you had wagered on 10, you can’t play the bonus. 

There are three of these additional games:

Cash Hunt

In the cash hunt, players are presented with a large screen of 108 multipliers. These numbers are covered by random symbols and then shuffled. Afterwards, each player gets to shoot at one symbol, and they receive the multiplier hidden beneath.

Coin Flip

Just as its name sounds, a coin takes centre stage in this bonus round. This coin has a blue and red side, and each will be assigned a multiplier. The coin is then flipped, and players win the multiplier that’s attached to the side that wins. 

Pachinko

When this game round starts, you’ll find a Pachinko wall with multiple pegs and a multiplier section below. The host drops a puck into this wall, and players win whatever multiplier it lands on. If it hits a “Double,” the multiplier values will be doubled. The host keeps dropping the puck until it hits an actual multiplier. 

Crazy Time

This is the highlight of the game and the bonus round that can get you the maximum 20,000x multiplier. Once this game starts, you’re presented with a large money wheel with 64 segments. Most segments have multipliers, while a few are marked Double or Triple. 

Once the puck lands on the multiplier, the game round ends. However, if it lands on a Double or Triple, the values are doubled or tripled, and the wheel is spun again. The process repeats until a multiplier is won.
