How to Play Funky Time Live Casino Game at Casino Days

Funky Time is played on a large wheel divided into segments. Players bet on where the wheel will land, and the host spins the wheel. They win if the wheel lands on the segment the player selected. Each round is short, but it can offer some decent rewards.

There are 64 segments, and the more of one type of segment there is, the better the chance the wheel will land there. To make a bet, select your bet size and click on the bets you want to make.

  1. Bet: Choose your stake level and place your bet.
  2. Bet Types: Players can bet on the 28 segments marked with a number 1, players can place bets on the letters that make up Play, Funk, and Time, or you can place a bet on one of the bonus rounds. There are 4 bonus rounds: Bar, Stayin’ Alive, Disco, and VIP Disco.
  3. Rewards: You receive a payout if the wheel lands on the number or letter bet. If the wheel ends on a bonus game with your bet, you will enter the chosen mini-game.

Bonus Rounds/Games

There are four bonus rounds in Funky Time, where you can potentially find huge multipliers.

Bar Bonus

The Bar Bonus has 6 segments on the wheel. Entering this bonus round will take you to a new screen where a funky bar and robot are waiting to greet you. All you have to do is pick one of the glasses filled with multipliers. The bartender will pour your drink to reveal the multiplier. Also, one reel slot spins on the robot’s face and randomly awards another multiplier to one of the glasses.

Stayin’ Alive

This appears only twice on the wheel and is a colour game inspired by bingo. You must pick a colour that becomes your team. Three coloured balls advance up a multiplier. A ball drawing machine is then used to determine whether the multiplier of the ball advances. When a black ball is drawn, the team loses a life, and once three lives are lost, the game is over.

Disco

The Disco bonus appears in three sections of the wheel. The game shows 37 virtual dance floor squares, with Mr Funky dancing away. A mini wheel is spun, which decides which way Mr Funky goes, collecting multipliers on the way. If Mr Funky falls off the floor, the game ends, and the multipliers are added together.

VIP Disco

This bonus game works the same way as Disco, but it offers bigger rewards and is only on one section of the wheel.
