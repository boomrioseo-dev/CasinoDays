Bonus Rounds/Games

If the wheel stops on 2 rolls or 4 rolls, Mr Monopoly will take you on an exciting adventure on the animated 3D Monopoly board. To participate in the bonus game, you must have bet on the spot where the wheel landed. If you didn’t, you simply watch the action unfold.

Dice are rolled by the presenter using an automatic shaker. Evolution ensures you get a close-up look so you can see the result of each roll. After the roll, Mr Monopoly steps on the board and covers the squares equivalent to the total dice number. He does this until he has covered the number of rolls (2 or 4, depending on where the wheel had stopped). 

After the bonus round, Mr Monopoly checks the space he ended up in. Players then get whatever price that section is worth.
